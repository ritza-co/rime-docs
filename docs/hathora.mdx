[Hathora](https://hathora.dev/) provides comprehensive compute orchestration by aggregating CPUs or GPUs for high performance, low latency, real-time applications.

Rime's text-to-speech (TTS) synthesis model is available through the Hathora. With Hathora's Rime integration, you can develop responsive AI voice applications that deliver natural, lifelike interactions.

It’s easy to get started running model inference for Rime’s [Arcana TTS model](https://www.rime.ai/blog/arcana-v2/) on the Hathora platform. 

Just follow the steps outlined in [Hathora's documentation](https://hathora.dev/docs/inference/deploying-a-model).
